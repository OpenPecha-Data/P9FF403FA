sāmantakena |

ye kuḍyasyābhyantare bhikṣavaḥ prativasanti tair ekadhye niṣadya poṣadhaḥ kartavyaḥ pravāraṇā
jñaptiḥ jñaptidvitīyaṃ jñapticarturthaṃ karma kartavyaṃ |

vyagrāḥ kurvanti sātisārā bhavanti |
