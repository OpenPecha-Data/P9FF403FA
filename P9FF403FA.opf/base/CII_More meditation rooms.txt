prapātaṃ bhavati |bhagavān āha | vedikā parikṣeptavyā |

tat kaṃpate |bhagavān āha | lohakaṇṭakaiḥ kīlayitavyā |

tathāpi na māpayanti |bhagavān āha | bahir vā layanapaṅktiḥ kartavyā |
